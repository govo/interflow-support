Intermittent Flow - Technical Support

What is Intermittent Flow?

Intermittent Flow is an innovative focus method that helps users maintain long-term high efficiency through scientifically designed micro-breaks. Compared to traditional time management methods, Intermittent Flow better aligns with the natural rhythm of human attention.

Why Choose Intermittent Flow?

Advantages over traditional Pomodoro Technique:

  • Better aligned with natural attention rhythm (Ultradian Rhythm)
  • Doesn't interrupt "flow state", but gently embeds micro-recovery
  • Improves sustainability of 90-minute focus sessions
  • Eliminates attention collapse from prolonged "pushing through"
  • More human-friendly and enjoyable focus experience

Why Intermittent Flow is Perfect for AI Collaboration:

  • Helps maintain clear thinking and prevents AI output fatigue
  • Micro-breaks allow for organizing thoughts and optimizing prompts
  • Regular breaks help maintain critical thinking about AI outputs
  • Prevents attention decline and judgment impairment from prolonged work

Special Recommendations for AI Work:

  • Review and reflect on AI output quality during micro-breaks
  • Use break time to organize and optimize prompts
  • Maintain appropriate distance to avoid over-reliance on AI
  • Protect your eyes by avoiding prolonged screen time
  • Engage in physical activity during long breaks to keep your mind active

Workflow Details

1. Focus Cycle (90 minutes)

Each complete focus cycle is 90 minutes, which is scientifically proven to be the optimal focus duration. During this period:

  • The system triggers a micro-break prompt every 3-5 minutes randomly
  • Maintain focus state on current task
  • When hearing the prompt sound, prepare for micro-break

2. Micro-break (8-10 seconds)

Micro-break is the core feature of Intermittent Flow:

  • Duration: 8-10 seconds (random)
  • Suggested actions:
    • Close eyes and take deep breaths
    • Relax your eyes
    • Stretch your body
  • Sound cues: Gentle start and return prompt sounds

3. Long Break (20 minutes)

After completing the 90-minute focus cycle:

  • The system plays a positive, longer prompt sound
  • Enter a 20-minute complete rest period
  • Suggested to engage in appropriate physical activity or relaxation

Usage Recommendations

  • Choose a quiet, comfortable environment
  • Prepare necessary items in advance to avoid interruptions
  • Truly relax during micro-breaks, don't continue working
  • Maintain regular sleep schedule
  • Adjust focus duration according to personal circumstances

Important Notes

  • This workflow makes it easy to achieve 90-minute focus, but don't force yourself to complete the full cycle—take a break whenever you feel uncomfortable.
  • Don't check phones or engage in distracting activities during micro-breaks
  • Maintain adequate water intake
  • Pay attention to maintaining correct posture
  • Extend rest time if feeling fatigued

Applicable Scenarios

  • Deep Work
  • Study and Exam Preparation
  • Music Practice
  • Creative Writing
  • Activities requiring sustained flow state

间歇流 - 技术支持

什么是间歇流？

间歇流是一种创新的专注方法，它通过科学设计的微休息机制，帮助用户在更自然的节奏中维持长时间的高效专注。与传统的时间管理方法相比，间歇流更符合人类注意力的自然波动规律。

为什么选择间歇流？

相比传统番茄工作法的优势：

  • 更符合人类注意力自然波动节律（Ultraadian Rhythm）
  • 不打断"专注感"，而是温和嵌入微恢复
  • 提高90分钟专注的可持续性
  • 消除长时间"撑着"导致的注意力崩溃
  • 更人性化、更友好的专注体验

为什么间歇流特别适合AI协同工作：

  • 帮助保持清晰的思维，避免AI输出疲劳
  • 微休息期间可以整理思路，优化提示词
  • 定期休息有助于保持对AI输出的批判性思考
  • 避免长时间工作导致的注意力下降和判断力减弱

AI工作时的特别建议：

  • 在微休息时回顾和思考AI的输出质量
  • 利用休息时间整理和优化提示词
  • 保持适当的距离感，避免过度依赖AI
  • 注意保护眼睛，避免长时间盯着屏幕
  • 在长休息时进行适当的身体活动，保持思维活跃

工作流程详解

1. 专注周期（90分钟）

每个完整的专注周期为90分钟，这是经过科学验证的最佳专注时长。在这期间：

  • 系统会每3-5分钟随机触发一次微休息提示
  • 保持专注状态，专注于当前任务
  • 当听到提示音时，准备进入微休息

2. 微休息（8-10秒）

微休息是间歇流的核心特色：

  • 时长：8-10秒（随机）
  • 建议动作：
    • 闭眼深呼吸
    • 放松眼睛
    • 舒展身体
  • 提示音：轻柔的开始提示音和回归提示音

3. 长休息（20分钟）

完成90分钟专注周期后：

  • 系统会播放一段积极、稍长的提示音
  • 进入20分钟的完整休息时间
  • 建议进行适当的身体活动或放松

使用建议

  • 选择安静、舒适的环境
  • 提前准备好所需物品，避免中途打断
  • 在微休息时真正放松，不要继续工作
  • 保持规律的作息时间
  • 根据个人情况调整专注时长

注意事项

  • 使用本工作流可以轻松达到90分钟专注，但不要强迫自己完成整个周期，如果感到不适可以适当休息
  • 微休息时不要看手机或进行其他分心活动
  • 保持充足的水分摄入
  • 注意保持正确的坐姿
  • 如果感到疲劳，可以适当延长休息时间

适用场景

  • 深度工作（Deep Work）
  • 学习与备考
  • 音乐练习
  • 创意写作
  • 需要持续心流的活动
  • AI协同工作（如提示词工程、AI编程等）

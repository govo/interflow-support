InterFLow Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

1. Introduction

InterFLow is an application focused on helping users improve their learning efficiency. We take your privacy and data security very seriously. This privacy policy aims to explain how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Data Collection

InterFLow only collects the following necessary information:

  • Task content you create
  • Task completion status
  • Task type classification

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device, and we do not upload your data to any servers. This means:

  • All your data is saved on your device
  • We do not collect or store your personal information
  • Your data is not shared with any third parties

4. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally, your data security primarily depends on your device security. We recommend that you:

  • Regularly backup your device data
  • Ensure your device has appropriate access protection
  • Keep the application updated to receive the latest security updates

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Delete the application and all its data at any time
  • Export your task data (if the application provides this feature)
  • Modify or delete tasks you have created at any time

6. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you within the application.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: govomusic@gmail.comp
